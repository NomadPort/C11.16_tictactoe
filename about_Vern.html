About Me

background

I have a BS degree in aerospace engineering and my career consists of 16 years working in that industry (Lockheed Martin and Boeing). I was laid off in 2011 and the past 5 years I have been a stay-at-home dad for three small kids while my wife works. As part of my college education, I learned programming languages (FORTRAN, Pascal) and got exposed to others (C, C++, HTML) while working. After graduating, I programmed via FORTRAN an orbital mechanics program that was used by my professor's students.

why i'm taking this class

Initially, I wanted to get back into the aerospace industry the past year, but it has been tough as the industry has not ramped up as I had hoped. Of all the alternatives, becoming a web developer seemed the most prudent. There seems to be a need for them, the pay isn't bad, and I liked programming in school. I like the challenge of solving a problem through math, logic and utilizing the computer's resources (doing operations very fast and doing things repetitively without complaining). I never programmed professionally and I imagine that there are differences between the programming languages I took in the past and current web development languages. I don't want to spend alot of time getting another bachelor's degree, so it seems a bootcamp is the right course. After checking a couple different bootcamps, LearningFuze seems to be the right fit.

the job i want

I think the first job I want is a front-end web developer. I want to know the design principles, rules of thumb, etc. that go into creating a well-designed website and I want to practice them. After that, I'm not sure as it would be presumptuous of me to know all the different specialties within web development. As an end goal, I would become a master and my reputation would allow to select which projects to work on both at work and on my own.

why i'm hungry for that job

Starting out, I want to point to some website and say "That's my creation or I worked on that part of it." That'll be great motivation, especially in the beginning. Also, I need to start working so that my kids know that I can and will work.
