Rules for Tic Tac Toe

  • 1st player is X.

  • 1st player chooses 1 of the 9 squares.

  • 2nd player chooses 1 of the remaining empty squares.

  • Play then returns to 1st player and play alternates between 1st and 2nd player.

  • If a player gets 3 Xs or 3 Os in a row either in a straight line or diagonally, that player wins.

  • If no player wins when all 9 square are filled in, then the game is a tie or draw.
